11.7 Provide access to safe and inclusive green & public space

Open and green spaces offer opportunities to advance wellbeing and a sense of personal satisfaction for all individuals living in urban areas. Improving our physical and mental wellbeing, fortifying our networks, and causing our urban areas and neighborhoods to become progressively alluring spots to live in and work are some of the advantages.

Solution

Provide universal access to safe, inclusive and accessible, green and public spaces, in particular for women and children, older persons and persons with disabilities.
