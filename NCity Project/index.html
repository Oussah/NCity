11. Sustainable Cities
and Communities

As a future generation it is important to know what is planned for our future world and know how we could be part of it.

Overview

Susatainable Cities and Communities is one of the 17 UN's SDG goal. This 11th goal is to make cities and human settlement, safe, resilient and sustainable. There are targets they set and here we raise 3 targets, that are most convineint to everyone, to show and encourage people to engage more so that we all can help make a change.

Affordable and sustainable transport systems:

Indicator 11.2.1 is the “proportion of population that has convenient access to public transport by sex, age and persons with disabilities.”

Reduce the environmental impact of cities:

Indicator 11.6.1: Proportion of urban solid waste regularly collected and with adequate final discharge out of total urban solid waste generated, by cities.

Provide access to safe and inclusive green & public space:

Indicator 11.7.1 is the "average share of the built-up area of cities that is open space for public use for all, by sex, age and persons with disabilities".
